Let's make sure our holiday waste is handled responsibly! Remember to **reduce, reuse, and recycle** this season. Focus on these main categories:

  • **Paper & Cardboard:** Flatten boxes and remove all tape and non-paper items.
  • **Plastics (1-7):** Rinse bottles and containers completely clean before placing them in the bin.
  • **Metals:** Aluminum cans and clean steel food cans are highly valuable for recycling.

Check local guidelines for proper disposal of specialized materials like string lights or batteries.
